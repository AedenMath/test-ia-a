# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for all members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political belief, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

We expect all community members to treat each other with respect and dignity, fostering an environment where everyone feels safe to participate, contribute, and grow.

## Expected Behavior

### Be Respectful
- Treat all community members with courtesy and respect
- Listen actively to others' perspectives and ideas
- Acknowledge and learn from different viewpoints
- Disagree gracefully and constructively

### Be Inclusive
- Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- Use inclusive language and avoid assumptions
- Make efforts to help beginners and newcomers feel welcome
- Actively work to include underrepresented or marginalized voices

### Be Honest and Transparent
- Communicate clearly and honestly in all interactions
- Take responsibility for your mistakes and learn from them
- Provide constructive feedback with good intentions
- Be open about your limitations and uncertainties

### Be Collaborative
- Work together toward shared goals
- Support and celebrate the contributions of others
- Share knowledge and help less experienced members
- Give credit where it is due

### Be Professional
- Keep discussions focused and on-topic
- Use clear, professional language in all communications
- Respect others' time and contributions
- Maintain confidentiality when appropriate

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

### Harassment and Discrimination
- Verbal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Harassment based on protected characteristics (race, gender, religion, disability, etc.)
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Intimidation or threats of any kind

### Exclusionary Behavior
- Deliberately excluding people from conversations or activities
- Gatekeeping or belittling others' contributions
- Making assumptions about others' capabilities based on identity
- Creating an unwelcoming environment for any group

### Harmful Content
- Posting or sharing content that is abusive, hateful, or violent
- Spreading misinformation or disinformation
- Sharing private information without consent (doxxing)
- Promoting illegal activities

### Disruptive Behavior
- Spam or off-topic posts
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Excessive self-promotion
- Abuse of community tools or systems

### Professional Misconduct
- Violation of intellectual property rights
- Plagiarism or unauthorized use of others' work
- Conflicts of interest not disclosed
- Misuse of community resources

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including but not limited to:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Issue and pull request comments
- Code reviews
- Community chat channels and forums
- In-person and virtual events
- Official social media accounts
- Any communication on behalf of the community

This Code of Conduct also applies to conduct outside of community spaces if it has the potential to adversely affect the safety and well-being of community members.

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by:

1. **Direct Communication**: If comfortable, you may directly address the behavior with the person involved
2. **Private Report**: Contact the project maintainers directly via email or private message
3. **Formal Report**: Submit a detailed report including:
   - Description of the incident
   - Participants involved (if known)
   - Date, time, and location
   - Any relevant context or evidence
   - Your contact information (optional)

All reports will be:
- Treated confidentially
- Investigated promptly and fairly
- Handled with sensitivity and discretion

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- A private warning or educational conversation
- A public warning or request to modify behavior
- Temporary suspension of community privileges
- Permanent removal from the community

The specific response will depend on the nature and severity of the violation, history of previous violations, and the impact on the community.

### Appeals

Community members who believe they have been unfairly treated can appeal decisions by contacting the project maintainers with a detailed explanation of their concerns.

## Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers and community leaders are responsible for:
- Modeling inclusive and respectful behavior
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Responding promptly to reports of violations
- Protecting the confidentiality of reporters
- Creating a safe environment for all community members
- Providing support and resources to those affected by violations

## Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1, available at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2_1/code_of_conduct.html.

## Questions?

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct or need clarification on any aspect, please reach out to the project maintainers. We're here to help create a welcoming and inclusive community for everyone.
